About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences advised by Murat Arcak. I am broadly interested in control, optimization, dynamical systems, statistics and machine learning. My research focuses on developing scalable algorithms for optimal control, uncertainty propagation, and statistical estimation in dynamic systems. I apply these algorithms to solve problems in medical imaging and automated anesthesia.
